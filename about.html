Meet Coach Marilyn Dogan

With over 15 years of experience helping individuals unlock their potential, I've dedicated my life to empowering others to create lasting, meaningful change.

A Bit More About Me



From leading hurricane recovery efforts in the U.S. Virgin Islands to creating youth programs like Smooth Talkers at the YMCA, my journey has been rooted in service, leadership, and transformation.

I trained with Landmark Education and the Jack Canfield Group, completed the Excellence in Government Fellowship, and served in management roles at the SBA and YMCA. I’m also proud to be a Texas Southern University alum—where I once competed in Athens, Greece as part of the Debate Team! I believe everyone has boundless possibilities. Let’s rise together.
