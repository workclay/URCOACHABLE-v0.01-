About

Services

UR Coachable offers a holistic range of coaching and planning services designed to support personal growth, meaningful experiences, and intentional success. From one-on-one life coaching and immersive weekend retreats to flexible online training, vacation planning, party planning, and business development support, each service is thoughtfully tailored to meet individual needs. Whether you are seeking clarity, renewal, celebration, or strategic direction, UR Coachable provides structured guidance and personalized solutions that help you move forward with confidence and purpose.


Commitment of Participants


  • Keep every coaching call at the time designated (it is the participant’s responsibility to call the coach at the given appointment time)

  • Keep their word Even if they disagree with the course of action the coach is espousing, they will follow the directive given

  • Give 100% of themselves

  • Be totally transparent

  • Continue to strive to have a life that is so empowered and is an inspiration to others


    • Commitment of Coach


  • Listen to the participant from their greatness not their problem

  • Challenge the participant to go past their comfort zone

  • Help the participant to develop a strategy that taps into their inner power and greatness

  • Envision the best possible solution

  • Be the participant’s greatest advocate

  • Help participant to identify blind spots and strategies to overcome lifelong behavior that has sabotaged their greatness

  • Help the participant develop strategies so that their life occurs as a joy, adventure and exciting time.

    • Coaching

    UR Coachable offers one-on-one life coaching over a six-week time span. Weekly sessions are scheduled usually 20 to 30 minutes per session.  During these sessions the participant would complete the assignment and directives given in a previous call and comes prepared to discuss challenges and/or accomplishments. At the end of the six-week sessions, participants have testified to have a new sense of accomplishment and freedom.

    Workshops

    UR Coachable will offer workshops that address various audiences to include - single parents, unfocused teens, retirees, individuals changing careers, individuals coping with death and individuals that want better leadership and communication skills.

    Weekend Retreats

    UR Coachable offers immersive weekend retreats designed to provide focused personal growth in a supportive, distraction-free environment. Participants engage in guided workshops, reflective exercises, and group discussions that encourage clarity, healing, and goal alignment. By the end of the retreat, attendees often leave feeling reenergized, grounded, and equipped with actionable insights for everyday life.

    Online Training

    UR Coachable provides structured online training programs that allow participants to grow at their own pace from anywhere. Each module includes guided lessons, practical assignments, and reflective exercises designed to build awareness and confidence. Upon completion, participants commonly report increased clarity, motivation, and practical tools they can immediately apply.

    Vacation Planning

    UR Coachable offers intentional vacation planning services that focus on rest, balance, and personal fulfillment rather than stress. Each plan is customized to align with the client’s goals for relaxation, exploration, or personal renewal. Clients often return from their experience feeling refreshed, focused, and more connected to themselves.

    Party Planning

    UR Coachable provides thoughtful party planning services that transform special occasions into meaningful, well-organized experiences. From concept development to execution, each event is designed with intention, flow, and personal significance. Hosts frequently report reduced stress and greater enjoyment, while guests leave with lasting memories.

    Business Development

    UR Coachable offers personalized business development coaching focused on clarity, strategy, and sustainable growth. Through structured sessions and targeted action steps, clients refine their vision, strengthen decision-making, and address operational challenges. By the end of the process, participants often experience increased confidence, direction, and measurable progress in their business goals.
